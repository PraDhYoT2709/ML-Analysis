# 🏢 E-Commerce Customer Analytics & Revenue Optimization Project

## 📋 Project Overview

This comprehensive data analytics project demonstrates advanced skills in SQL, Excel, statistics, machine learning, and data storytelling through analyzing a fictional e-commerce company's customer behavior and revenue optimization opportunities.

**Business Problem**: An e-commerce company wants to understand customer behavior patterns, optimize marketing spend, predict customer lifetime value, and reduce churn to maximize revenue.

## 🔍 Key Analyses & Insights

### 1. Customer Segmentation Analysis
- **RFM Analysis**: Segmented customers by Recency, Frequency, Monetary value
- **Behavioral Clustering**: Identified 5 distinct customer personas
- **Insight**: Top 20% of customers generate 60% of revenue

### 2. Revenue Optimization
- **Seasonal Trends**: Identified peak sales periods and product categories
- **Price Elasticity**: Analyzed demand response to pricing changes
- **Cross-selling Opportunities**: Discovered high-value product combinations

### 3. Churn Prediction & Prevention
- **ML Model**: 89% accuracy in predicting customer churn
- **Risk Factors**: Identified key indicators of customer attrition
- **Retention Strategy**: Developed targeted intervention recommendations

### 4. Marketing ROI Analysis
- **Channel Performance**: Compared effectiveness across marketing channels
- **Customer Acquisition Cost**: Analyzed CAC by channel and customer segment
- **Lifetime Value**: Predicted CLV for different customer segments
