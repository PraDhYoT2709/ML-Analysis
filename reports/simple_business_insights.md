# 📊 Customer Analytics Business Insights
## Executive Summary & Strategic Recommendations

**Project Goal:** Understand customer behavior to increase sales by 15% within 6 months

**Analysis Period:** Current customer base of 1,000 customers with 2,285 orders

---

## 🎯 Key Findings

### 📈 **Business Performance Overview**
- **Customer Conversion Rate:** 79.7% (797 of 1,000 customers have made purchases)
- **Average Order Value:** $97.71
- **Total Revenue:** $223,274
- **Revenue per Customer:** $223.27

### 🔍 **Critical Discovery: All Customers Are At-Risk**
Our analysis revealed that **100% of customers** are classified as "At-Risk" due to high recency (average 302 days since last order). This indicates a **major retention crisis** that requires immediate action.

## 📊 Statistical Insights

### 👥 **Customer Demographics**
- **Average Age:** 35.2 years (range: 18-72)
- **Gender Split:** 53.6% Female, 46.4% Male
- **No significant spending differences** between genders (p=0.95)

### 📱 **Channel Performance Ranking**
1. **Mobile App:** 81.5% conversion rate, $98.56 avg order value

4. **Website:** 78.5% conversion rate, $99.40 avg order value
5. **Referral:** 77.1% conversion rate, $93.03 avg order value

### 🎯 **Age-Based Spending Patterns**

- **36-45 years:** $96.71 average
- **46+ years:** $91.50 average (lowest spending group)

## 🚨 Critical Business Issues

### **1. Retention Crisis**
- **Problem:** Average 302 days since last customer order
- **Impact:** High churn risk across entire customer base
- **Root Cause:** Lack of engagement and retention strategies

### **2. Missed Revenue Opportunity**  
- **Problem:** 20.3% of customers (203) have never made a purchase
- **Impact:** $20,000+ in potential lost revenue
- **Solution:** Immediate conversion campaigns needed
